Favorite Book


  • Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/dp/1444758993/

By far, one of my favorite book series is Red Rising by Pierce Brown. It currently is a six book series with one final book in the works. While it may start out like a Young Adult series with the first book, the second book and beyond are nothing short of a masterpieces.

It is a science fiction book, fast-paced, and beautifully written characters and events.
